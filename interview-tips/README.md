## Quick Tips

- **Clarify before coding** — Repeat the problem, ask edge-case questions.
- **Think aloud** — Interviewers want to understand your thought process.
- **Start with brute force** — Then optimize; this shows you can always get to a working solution.
- **Talk about complexity** — Always state time and space complexity.
- **Test your code** — Walk through examples, including edge cases.
- **Stay calm** — If you're stuck, narrate what you're thinking; hints come to those who communicate.
